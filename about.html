Couples Resort

ABOUT

Ontario’s Couples Resort is on the shores of Algonquin Park’s Galeairy Lake, nestled in 100 acres of Algonquin Park, on the traditional lands of the Algonquin People.

Luxury Resort for Couples

A 5-STAR Luxury Resort for all couples. Endless nature and adventure await, alongside gourmet dining, spa treatments, and relaxing lakeside escapes.

Lakeside Patio & Pool

  • Service Patio for music, drinks, and snacks
  • Outdoor heated pool open year-round

Gift Shop & Rec Room

  • 3 slate billiard tables
  • 1 ping pong table
  • 2 dartboards

Gym & Sports Center

  • Rowing machines and treadmills
  • Sauna and free weights
  • Motorboats, kayaks, and canoes
